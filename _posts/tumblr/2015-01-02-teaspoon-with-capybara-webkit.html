Do you use teaspoon to run your javascript tests?

Do you use Capybara-Webkit to run your feature tests?

Now you can run your teaspoon suite faster without having to install and boot up phantomjs. Just use the new Capybara-Webkit driver for teaspoon! You may also have more success with it on your CI server, as that is what caused us to make this change.
